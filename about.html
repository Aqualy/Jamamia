Our food truck is called “Jamamia”, a food truck that gives an original twist to the classic Italian pizza. We offer a wide range of small pizzas, from authentic classics to modern creations, with a focus on fresh, high-quality ingredients. Our food truck is intended for young and old, and we want to offer visitors a fun and unforgettable experience.
